OpAuto ERP — AI-Powered Features Brainstorm
=============================================

1. AI Diagnostic Assistant (Chatbot)
   Mechanic inputs symptoms (strange noise, vibration, warning light code) → AI suggests
   probable causes, required parts, and estimated repair time. Uses Claude API.
   This is the hero AI feature — very domain-specific and visually impressive.

2. RAG-Powered Technical Knowledge Base
   Upload repair manuals, technical bulletins, OEM docs → mechanics ask natural language
   questions → get answers with source citations.
   Example: "How do I replace the timing belt on a 2019 Peugeot 308?"

3. Predictive Maintenance Alerts
   Based on car history, mileage, and manufacturer intervals → AI predicts when each car
   needs its next service. Dashboard shows "3 cars due for brake inspection this month."
   Classic predictive model showcase.

4. Smart Scheduling Optimizer
   AI suggests optimal appointment slots considering: mechanic skills, job duration estimates,
   workload balancing, and bay availability. Drag an appointment → AI warns "This mechanic
   is overloaded, suggest moving Job X to 3pm."

5. AI Analytics Narrator
   Instead of just showing charts, AI generates natural language insights:
   "Revenue is up 15% this month, driven by bodywork repairs. Tuesdays are your busiest day.
   Mechanic Ahmed has the highest completion rate."
   Turns data into storytelling.

6. Intelligent Invoice Generation
   Auto-generate invoice line items and descriptions from a job card. AI suggests pricing
   based on job type, parts used, and labor time. Detects anomalies: "This brake job is
   priced 40% below your average — confirm?"

7. Image-Based Damage Assessment (Computer Vision)
   Customer uploads car damage photos → AI estimates damage severity, suggests repair type
   (bodywork vs. paint only), and gives rough cost estimate. Very visual and impressive
   for a demo.

8. Customer Churn Prediction & Smart Segmentation
   AI clusters customers by behavior (visit frequency, spend, car age) → identifies VIPs,
   at-risk customers, and upsell opportunities.
   "Customer Dupont hasn't visited in 6 months — send a reminder?"

9. Demand Forecasting for Inventory
   Predict parts demand based on seasonal patterns, upcoming appointments, and car types
   in the fleet. "Order 20 oil filters — 12 oil changes scheduled next week + seasonal trend."
   Classic time-series prediction.

10. AI-Powered Work Order Copilot
    When creating a maintenance job, AI auto-suggests: related jobs to bundle ("Add wheel
    alignment? 70% of tire changes include it"), required parts from inventory, and estimated
    duration. Speeds up the workflow.


Bonus Ideas
-----------
- Voice-to-Work-Order — speech recognition to create jobs hands-free
- Sentiment analysis on customer notes/feedback
- Anomaly detection on financial transactions


Recommended Core Four (maximum impact)
---------------------------------------
#1 + #2 + #3 + #5 — covers chatbot, RAG, predictive models, and generative AI.
